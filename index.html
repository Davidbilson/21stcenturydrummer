GUIDE TO BECOMING A GOOD STANDING DRUMMER



Encountering tough challenges is one part of the process of growing to become a great drummer and being a great drummer and being and being a great drummer requires conquering these challenges. Sometimes, i get questions like "why is my left hand so weak and how can i build it up to balance with the right hand?"..."why do bands complain of how much they roll which might seem embarassing"...then i talk to them about 'drum phrasing'   which helps a drummer to play neatly and become acceptable by bands or artistes.

I will like you to know that as an upcoming drummer, i had a lot of challenges ranging from my posture on drums, sticks handling, balancing my rudiments and the energy while playing. There was a day i had to be replaced while drumming becasue i was driving the music towards the dark end (playing like a weak man), it was such an embarrassing moment, i had to deal with it and i started all sort of research in order to be a drummer of my dream. I came across a lot of tips that helped my career in drumming after a lot of research, all i had to do was study fervently and practice while i also relate with my mentor.

Quick tips for beginner drummers



  • ✓ Practicing a single lesson learnt for 15 minutes is better than 1 hour of rough practice

  • ✓ Don't rush yourself to speed and don't try to be like any other drummer, be you!

  • ✓ Ensure your weak hand gets more work

  • ✓ Do not be discouraged when a practice is not yielding results over time

  • ✓ Always take a good rest

  • ✓ Listen to all genres of music

  • ✓ Go hard on your mentor, but don't stress them

  • ✓ Attend gigs and try to get a seat at the front

  • ✓ Practice witha metronome

  • ✓ Ensure your drum set is comfortable to play

  • ✓ Sit with correct drumming posture
